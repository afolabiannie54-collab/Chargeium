OUR MISSION

Chargeium exists to make meaning wearable. We believe clothes should hold memory, not just design. What we create is simple — but it carries energy. For us, fashion is about connection: between fabric and feeling, between who you are and who you’re becoming.

THE NAME "CHARGEIUM"

The name comes from charge — energy, motion, power — and –ium, the suffix most elements on the periodic table share. Together, it forms Chargeium: a made-up element that stands for creating your own energy, your own element.
